Who is Sarah Sharif ?

Earth native, Pakistani-born, British, Californian with a Master’s in Communication from St. Edward’s University and Bachelors in Biological Sciences from University of California, Davis. I’ve dabbled in biomedical engineering research (Louie Lab), environmental nonprofit work at Sierra Club and now focusing on projects focused on human-centered business design. From directing a Cancer Health Fair after learning about the biology behind cancer to running townhalls on the importance of air quality across My goal has always been to simply change the world for the best. I’ve directed ATX Hack for Change, an annual civic hackathon, here in Austin that launched in 2013. Under my direction, I was energized to take the event to the next level. I feel strongly that there needs to be a tremendous surge of creativity on a global scale for meaningful change and it all starts with playfulness. And I’m here to play.
